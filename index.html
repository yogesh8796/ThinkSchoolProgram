The heaven for travellers

Nomadic Townies is a website which lets you know how we managed the travellers to feel travel experience in different way! We believe the thrill of travelling should be matched by the excitement of arriving. If we love it… we’ll make sure you do too.

Nomadic travelling is great for people who want to explore the world and crave a sense of freedom and adventure.The most important tip to nomadic travelling with a yurt is to simply relax and enjoy the adventure. Getting caught up with unnecessary details will take away from the beautiful experience of living and travelling in a yurt. Downsizing and constantly moving may be overwhelming at first, but with the endless possibilities of where you can go, any concerns and doubts will seem greatly insignificant.
